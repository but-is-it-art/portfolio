St. Glitz

branding illustration graphic design

St. Glitz is a jewelry boutique inspired by the laid-back life in Marbella, Spain. Besides the carefully selected items, the space offers a DIY ambient where people can also have a pear soda and chill on the couch. Cozy, yet unpretentious.

The visual identity combines exotic bird silhouettes, vacation postcards and warm colors.

Project designed for Storience. Proposal not approved.
