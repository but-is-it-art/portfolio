The Monster Guide

illustration print design lettering

Either big or small, frightening or friendly, monsters change depending on how we perceive the world around us. They may help kids get over primordial fears or they may remain echoes of an adult's darkest thoughts. Inspired by Romanian folklore, "The Monster Guide" sets off to instruct both children and parents about the creatures that inhabit their homes and minds.

This book is my BA diploma project at UNArte, Graphic Design, class of '12.
