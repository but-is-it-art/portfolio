Cube Cake & Tea

illustration packaging design

Cube Cake is a small dessert studio in China where you can indulge in clafoutis and gooey cakes while having a cup of tea. The studio uses clean, local ingredients to offer their customers a nostalgic dessert experience, a getaway from the hustle and bustle of the modern city.

Because of their consistency, the cakes are packaged in reusable metal boxes. Flavours range from strawberry-velvet to oreo-brownie, each of them being associated with a color scheme and an animal.
