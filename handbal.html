Introduction To Handball

retouching illustration web design

Lead.ro promotes relevant, well-documented and well-written content that inspires readers through stories about people who never give up. The online publication recently launched a series of mini-sites that promotes different types of sports to the general public.

The first project from the series is a landing page dedicated to handball. The game strategy and player arrangement explanations are accompanied by video examples, making the information clear and accesible.

Project designed with Storience.
