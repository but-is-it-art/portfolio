Avenor College Campaign

retouching illustration web design

Avenor College is a non-profit, bilingual international school rooted in Romanian culture. The High School guides students to find their own strenghts, create their own journeys and frame their own futures.

In order to attract more applicants for the 2017-2018 admission process, we decided to focus on the students and their progress since attending Avenor College. The top 3 students from the High School became heroes in the campaign, each of them sharing their stories and vision about the future.

Project designed with Storience.
